About the Campsite

Completely Raw & Remote Lakeside Experience

Come visit our Igatpuri Lakeside Camping site in Marutiwadi — an isolated, remote escape completely out of the way of civilization. This is real hardcore camping like you've never experienced before.

Please note: The view is extremely amazing. We have very basic Indian-style common toilet facilities. Food is simple and tasty — made on a chulha just like village food. No electricity, only solar lights. You get a true moonlight camping experience. We do not have luxury — but you get something far more valuable.

Our campsite is very close to Mumbai, making it the perfect weekend escape. Every element here is designed for those who crave authentic nature immersion — not a resort.

Know Before You Go

Everything About Marutiwadi Lakeview Camping

Campsite Location & How to Reach

Address: Maruti Wadi Lake Side Camping, Wakapada, Khodala, Maruti Wadi Road, Sayade, Maharashtra 401604, India.

Near Igatpuri & Kasara. Approximately 50 km from Nashik. ~2.5 hrs from Mumbai.

After booking, location details on the voucher may vary. Guests are requested to call us in advance to get the exact camp location and directions. Our team will guide you right to the campsite entrance.

📞 Call us for GPS directions: +91 79773 93218

Adventure Activities We Offer

We offer a wide range of water sport and adventure activities at our lakeside campsite. All watersports include proper safety equipment and trained supervision.

  • Tent Stay / Camping
  • Kayaking
  • Raft Boating
  • Coracle Ride
  • Lake Fun Tubing
  • Lake Swimming with Life Jacket
  • Star Gazing
  • Night Trekking
  • Bonfire
  • BBQ Barbeque
  • Zipline
  • Rifle Shooting
  • Archery
  • Fun Team Activities

Monsoon Note: Camping near the lake is not possible in monsoon. Alternative accommodation is provided at a farmhouse area 5–6 km from the lake.

Things to Carry

  • ID Proof (Compulsory)
  • Sports or Trekking Shoes
  • Water Bottles
  • Dry Snacks
  • Personal First Aid / Prescription Medicines
  • Toiletries (napkin, face wash, tissue)
  • Backpack
  • Extra Pair of Clothes
  • Camera (Optional)
  • Torch with Extra Batteries
  • Bed Sheet (Optional)
  • Warm Clothes (Nights can be cool)

Tent Includes: Half-inch foam mattress, bedsheet & blanket.
Washrooms: 4 dedicated concrete Indian-style washrooms, cleaned before guests arrive.

General Advisory & Guidelines

  • Do not carry valuables or jewellery
  • Management not liable for lost belongings
  • No smoking inside tents
  • Remove footwear outside tents
  • No weapons or inflatable items on premises
  • Damage to property will be charged
  • Children below 5 years — FREE
  • Children 6–10 — Child prices apply
  • Above 10 years — Adult prices
  • Minimum 2 people required to book
  • Tents allocated on first-come basis
  • Rescheduling fee: 30% of booking value
  • Bonfire is common for all guests
  • Washrooms are common for all guests

🚫 Strictly Not Allowed: Drugs, Alcohol, Intoxicants, Weapons, or any Inflammable items. Violation results in immediate removal without refund.
